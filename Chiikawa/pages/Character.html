Chiikawa

Chiikawa is the main character who is caring and shy. This creature is described with white fur, small, bear-like ears and has a small, ball-shaped tail. It doesn't speak full sentences like its friend Hachiware but it can make sounds to express itself. Chiikawa always hangs out with its friends, Hachiware and Usagi.

Hachiware

Hachiware is a helpful and bright character. Well-known for their blue ears and tail, Hachiware can communicate well in the story while its friends aren't able to. This cat-like creature is the most extroverted of the trio. It is said by the author that Hachiware is not necessarily a cat.
